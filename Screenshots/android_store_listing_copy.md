# SANS 347 Android Store Listing Copy

## App Name
SANS 347

## Short Description
Categorize pressure equipment using SANS 347:2024 graphs, tables and modules.

## Full Description
SANS 347 is a practical engineering aid for pressure equipment categorization under SANS 347:2024 Edition 3.1.

Enter design pressure, volume or DN, equipment type, contents state and fluid group to calculate the applicable pressure equipment category. The app shows the category result, risk level, product value and the relevant SANS 347 figure with your equipment point plotted on the graph.

Use SANS 347 to:
- Categorize pressure vessels, piping and steam generators
- Calculate SEP, Category I, II, III, IV or Not Regulated results
- View the applicable SANS 347 graph with plotted result
- Browse all 9 categorization figures
- Reference conformity assessment modules for Categories I-IV
- Review key SANS 347 tables and professional review notes
- Export or share a text summary of your result

This app is an engineering reference tool. It does not replace the SANS 347 standard, formal conformity assessment, legal compliance review or professional engineering judgement.
